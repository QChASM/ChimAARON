Browse AaronTools Libraries

To browse the AaronTools Ligand, Substituent, and Ring Fragment libraries in Chimera, open the AaronTools Libraries menu under Tools → ChimAARON.

The Ligand Library can be sorted by ligand name, denticity, or the elements of coordinating atoms. You have the option to highlight the ligands key atoms.

Items in the Substituent Library can be sorted by name and conformer information. You can choose to show a ghost bond to see where the substituent would connect to a molecule.

Ring Fragments can be sorted by name only. You can choose to see the direction AaronTools will walk around the ring when it's being mapped onto another structure.

In the menu shown above, three ligands are selected. All of these coordinate to a catalyst center with two oxygen atoms (per the "Coordinating Elements" column).
