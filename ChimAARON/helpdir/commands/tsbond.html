tsbond

Usage:
tsbond [ selec atom-spec ] [ color color ] [ transparency precent ]

tsbond replaces the selected bonds (either in atom-spec or the current selection) with transparent cylinders. Alternatively, if two atoms are specified that are not bonded, these cylinders are drawn where the bond between these atoms would be. The keywords modify the appearance of these cylinders:

  • color (default byatom) - color of cylinders; may be Chimera named color, r,g,b[,a] tuple, or byatom.
    NOTE If you don't want to color the cylinders byatom, consider coloring the bond and setting its transparency with the color and transparency Chimera commands. An actual bond will move with the atoms in an animation, and tsbond cylinders will not.
  • transparency (default 50) - percent transparency of the cylinder
